CHARLIZE

These stylish Papillio mules with a cork footbed are clean, minimalist and exceptionally glamorous. The seamless upper on the Charlize model features an open selvedge finish and is made from a single piece of leather. It wraps around the footbed in the heel section, where it is stitched. The high-quality, soft smooth leather has been given a fashionable metallic coating.
